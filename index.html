About Me

Hello, I'm Alex, a passionate and curious individual with a diverse range of interests. Currently pursuing a Maths Degree and Physics Degree, I thrive on the pursuit of knowledge and enjoy exploring various fields of study. My natural curiosity has led me to delve into subjects such as science, philosophy, technology, and more.

With a background in tutoring mathematics and physics at all levels, as well as experience in the service industry as a waiter, I've developed excellent interpersonal skills and a strong ability to communicate complex ideas effectively. I find great joy in sharing my knowledge and helping others understand challenging concepts.

My true passion lies in the intersection of physics, philosophy, and technology. I aspire to work in the fields of Data Science and Artificial Intelligence, combining my analytical skills with my love for problem-solving. While obtaining a PhD in Physics is a long-term goal, I view it as more of a personal pursuit rather than a strictly professional endeavor.

I take pride in my notable achievements, including receiving an honors degree in high school within the scientific realm and being awarded the 2nd prize in a narrative high school writing contest. I've also overcome significant challenges in my personal life, including a generalized anxiety disorder, the loss of my father and distancing myself from extremist ideology groups.

In addition to my academic pursuits, I have a thirst for adventure and exploration. I have had the opportunity to travel to Sweden, Ireland, and the UK, immersing myself in different cultures and broadening my worldview.

I believe in lifelong learning and constantly seek new avenues to expand my knowledge. Somewhen, I am interested in mastering Mandarin Chinese, Modern Russian, and Modern Standard Arabic languages. Furthermore, I am committed to refining my existing skills and staying up-to-date with the latest advancements in my areas of interest.

In summary, my unwavering passion for learning and my multidisciplinary background make me a versatile and adaptable individual. I am eager to continue embracing new challenges, growing both personally and professionally, and making a positive impact on the world around me.
